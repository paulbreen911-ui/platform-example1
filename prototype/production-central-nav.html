Vendor rec

Best wireless IEM systems for outdoor festival use in high-RF environments?

Jordan K.2 hours ago22 replies
Running a 3-day outdoor festival next month and RF is going to be a nightmare. Anyone have experience with the Shure Axient Digital vs Sony DWX series in dense RF environments? We have about 24 channels of wireless running simultaneously with a TV truck on site. Trying to figure out which system is going to give us the best coordination tools and the cleanest signal in that scenario.
22 replies
TR
Tom R.FOH Engineer · 1h ago
Axient all the way for a scenario like that. The ShowLink remote monitoring alone is worth it when you're dealing with dense RF. We ran 32 channels of AD at a festival last summer with a broadcast truck and two neighboring stages and had zero drops all weekend. The Wireless Workbench coordination tools are unmatched.
ML
Mara L.2h ago
Second the Axient recommendation. Also — are you coordinating with the TV truck RF team in advance? That's the biggest variable. Getting their frequency list before you build your plan can save you a headache on site.
